Where Curiosity Meets Real-World Action

The Applied Economics & Impact Society at Cinco Ranch High School is a student-led club that blends academic curiosity with real-world action. Through research, outreach, and enterprise, members will explore how applied learnings intersects with business and community service to create impact.
